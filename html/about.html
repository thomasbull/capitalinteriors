Our History

Founded in 1975, Capital Interiors specialises in luxury interior design services for private residential and commercial clients. Our extensive international experience spans intimate cottages in the UK, palatial residences in the Middle and Far East, sumptuous residences in the Caribbean and private corporate offices in Europe.

Our Philosophy

Our business has grown mostly on reputation or through word-of-mouth recommendations. Each project benefits from our established approach of innovation, efficiency and attention to detail. We do not have a 'house style'. Instead, we confidently deliver our clients' requirements in their chosen style for each project. Our growth has been measured and calculated to provide and maintain a consistent and superior level of service to all of our clients on all projects.

Our Team

We have a dedicated team with hundreds of collective years of specialised experience in all aspects of the delivery of unique homes and projects. In addition, we have developed valuable working relationships with leading professional practices in every field of expertise, enabling us to provide the best advice and service to our clients.
