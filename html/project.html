Sophisticated Belgravia Residence

Situated in the heart of Belgravia, Capital Interiors designed, managed and contracted this turn-key project, completing in the summer of 2014. The finished Grade II listed home incorporates a basement leisure area, a multi-level rear extension, terrace and landscaped garden, together with a complete refurbishment of the existing house. The interiors throughout combine traditional and contemporary styles, retaining the property's period features alongside clean modern-lines and state-of-the-art facilities.
