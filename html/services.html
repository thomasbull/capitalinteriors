Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services, from interior design and project management through to bespoke furniture design. Contact us using the details below to find out more about how we could help with your project.

Interior Design & Architectural Services

Capital Interiors specialises in luxury properties and interiors for discerning clients. We have established an enviable reputation with our private clients and, in some instances, are working for the third generation of prominent families on both domestic and international projects.

Our proven experience covers all aspects of residential design, from cutting-edge apartments to English country houses. We pride ourselves on:

  • Understanding and interpreting clients' requirements
  • Tailoring style
  • Efficient delivery and cost management

Project Management & Turnkey Contracting

We see our projects through from inception to completion. We can serve as your sole point of contact throughout the process, dealing with, for example:

  • Securing planning permissions
  • Securing listed building consents
  • Managing contracts
  • Managing the professional team to oversee the entire project

Bespoke Finishes & Exclusive Furniture Design

In addition to offering a bespoke specialist joinery and fittings service, we arrange for the design and build of custom-made furniture. Close, long-established relationships with superior suppliers mean we can easily source the finest materials to create unique interiors with unparalled attention to detail. Some examples of furniture we have made for our clients include:

  • Bespoke cabinetry
  • Custom-built wine cellar
  • Feature lighting
  • His and hers walk-in wardrobes
